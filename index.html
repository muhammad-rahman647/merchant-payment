How It Works

Crypto Merchants

Powered by a FDIC-insured bank, Leisure Pay operates as a closed-loop blockchain payment ecosystem.

Customers can deposit funds into the FDIC-insured bank account using the payment of choice. They can then use the money to pay for their purchases. It's a credit card transaction with a tiny added step.

Fiat off ramp, Leisure Pay settles to any ATM instantly without the need for a physical card. We also provide the same-day ACH, WIRE, and instant usability with our Leisure Pay card options for fiat off-ramp services.
